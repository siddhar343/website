About us:

Shree Ganesh Jewellers, established in 1998, is a trusted jewelry store offering a wide range of gold, silver, and diamond ornaments. The shop specializes in traditional and modern jewelry designs including rings, necklaces, bangles, and bridal sets. All products are made using high-quality materials like 22K/24K gold and certified gemstones. Services such as jewelry repair, polishing, gold exchange, and custom design are also provided. .
